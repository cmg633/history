Bhangarh Fort with our guide

Bangarh Fort, nestled in the Aravalli Range of Rajasthan, India, is a site shrouded in mystery and intrigue. This abandoned fortress, believed to be haunted, carries with it an eerie aura that has attracted both historians and paranormal enthusiasts alike. Legends whisper tales of curses and tragic love stories, adding to its mystique. Despite its haunting reputation, the fort's architectural splendor stands as a testament to its glorious past, offering visitors a glimpse into a bygone era while igniting the imagination with its ghostly lore.
