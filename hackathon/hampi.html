Hampi with our guide

Hampi, located in the southern state of Karnataka, India, is a mesmerizing UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its rich history, captivating ruins, and stunning landscape. Once the majestic capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi now stands as a testament to a bygone era, with its sprawling temple complexes, intricate carvings, and grand monuments scattered across a surreal boulder-strewn terrain. Visitors to Hampi can immerse themselves in the echoes of ancient glory while exploring its fascinating architecture, vibrant bazaars, and serene riverside settings, making it a truly unforgettable destination for history buffs and travelers alike.
